Who We Are
Nature's Finest,
Crafted with Precision

Sree Krishna Botanicals is a premier manufacturer and global exporter of high-quality oleoresins, botanical spice extracts, standardised powders, and whole spices. Rooted in the fertile spice-growing heartlands of Andhra Pradesh, India, we combine traditional botanical knowledge with modern extraction technology to deliver products of unmatched purity, potency, and consistency.

Our state-of-the-art facility processes over 30 varieties of spices and herbs using food-grade solvent extraction, supercritical CO₂ extraction, and cold-press methods. Every batch is rigorously tested using HPLC and other analytical techniques before dispatch, ensuring that every product meets or exceeds global food safety and pharmaceutical standards.
